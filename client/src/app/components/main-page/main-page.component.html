Hospital Dashboard
"WeCare", a healthcare application, keeps your health at your hands. You can search hospitals near you. It helps you to book appointment with doctors depending on their available date and time.The app asks for your health issues/symptoms and accordingly suggest the hospital and department to be visited and shows the list of doctors available from the particular department.

Blood Bank
It also helps to get the details on blood groups available at blood-banks in near-by hospitals.

Notification
It would also give notifications based on the health camps organized by near-by hospitals.

User profile
It helps user to view his data evenever he needed. it shows all the details like username, email and password.
